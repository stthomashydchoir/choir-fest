About the Festival

A Celebration of Worship Across the Body of Christ

Selah 2026 is a one-day interdenominational church choir festival, bringing together voices from across denominations in a shared moment of worship, fellowship, and Spirit-filled song.

♪

St Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Hyderabad is proud to host this festival for the very first time on 18th April 2026 — opening its doors to choirs from across the region to gather, celebrate, and lift their voices together for His glory.

Our Church

St Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Hyderabad

Our story began around 1946 when Mar Thoma immigrants formed a Malayalee congregation in Hyderabad, worshipping at St. Andrews Church, Secunderabad. As the community grew, members around BHEL and Ramachandrapuram sought a worship centre closer to home — and through God’s providence, land was allotted at HUDA Colony, Chandanagar.

The foundation stone was laid on 2nd October 1986 and the church dedicated on 18th April 1987. In 2011 the parish was renamed Hyderabad St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, and in 2013 celebrated its Silver Jubilee by taking up home-for-homeless and marriage-aid projects. We are grateful to God for His faithfulness through every season.

Our Choir

The St Thomas Choir

The St Thomas Choir has been the heartbeat of worship at our parish for over thirty years. Comprising singers of all ages — from youth to seasoned voices — the choir leads the congregation in praise every Sunday and on special occasions throughout the liturgical year.
